Winners in each category will be announced and contacted by May 1st.

The following contest categories are defined as:



BEST MAP

Best overall map. Must take place in a plausibly World War II setting in Europe, North Africa, or the Eastern Front/Russia. Must be designed for Domination game mode

BEST BIG MAP

Best map designed for 64-100 players. Must be bigger than 5,000x5,000 units in editor.

STOCK ASSETS

Best map created using Days of War editor assets and UE4 Marketplace assets only, no custom art or geometry allowed other than textured BSP brushes or stock UE4 shapes

BEST LAYOUT

Best layout design. No assets allowed except for Vehicles, Sandbags, and other small props.



Judging Criteria

Final prizes will be awarded by Games Industry professionals from Driven Arts and other studios. Finalists will be selected with help from the community.

Judges will review each finalist based on the following criteria:

1. Design and Gameplay
2. Visual Quality
3. Creativity
4. Polish / Completeness

NOTE: Best Layout will not be judged on Visual Quality.


Contest Rules & Terms



All content submitted must be allowed to be distributed with Days of War. This means submissions must be completely original content, modified/unmodified versions of Driven Arts’ games or you must have full permission from the original author to use their content in this contest. And please don’t make one-to-one copies of maps from other games.

Your Map/Mod must work with the current version Days of War available via Steam and must be made available via Days of War's Steam Workshop.

All submissions to this contest must also be made publicly available using

To enter the contest, you must also complete the submission form, including your full contact details and a link to your submission on Steam Workshop. Finally, by entering the contest, you consent to your submission remaining available on Steam Workshop after the contest comes to a close.

The community will have an opportunity to choose the top entrants in each Round and Category, with the final judging being carried out by members of the development team at Driven Arts. Learn more about the judging criteria.

Cash prizes are in United States Dollars (USD) and are non-negotiable. Prize-winners are responsible for any or all related taxes incurred. Driven Arts is not obliged to award all the prizes if there is a lack of entries or entries are not of acceptable quality.

Please keep in mind that anything that is illegal, breaks the terms of our EULA or is otherwise offensive will not be allowed. Employees or contractors of Driven Arts are automatically disqualified.
