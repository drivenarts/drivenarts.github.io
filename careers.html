Job Listing - Animator with Proficiency in Unreal Engine (UE4/5)

Location: Remote
Type: Full-Time

Role Overview:

As an Animator at Driven Arts, you will play a crucial role in developing high-quality animations for characters, environments, and in-game assets. You will collaborate closely with our art and design teams to create animations that enhance gameplay and storytelling within Unreal Engine.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Create realistic and stylized animations for characters, creatures, and environmental assets.
  • Implement and optimize animations within Unreal Engine 4/5, ensuring seamless integration and performance.
  • Collaborate with the design team to develop animation concepts that align with gameplay mechanics and narrative.
  • Utilize UE4’s animation tools, including Sequencer and Blueprints, to create dynamic, interactive animations.
  • Participate in the rigging and skinning process, working closely with modelers to ensure assets are ready for animation.
  • Contribute to the creative process by providing input on game design and character development.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and advancements in animation technology, particularly within Unreal Engine.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of experience in animation, with a strong portfolio showcasing your work in Unreal Engine 4/5.
  • Proficiency in UE’s animation blueprints
  • Experience with character rigging and skinning.
  • Strong understanding of animation principles and techniques, with the ability to create both realistic and stylized animations.
  • Familiarity with motion capture integration and cleanup.
  • Excellent collaboration and communication skills, with the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
  • Creative problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • A passion for gaming and a strong desire to learn.

What We Offer:

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • A creative and inclusive work environment.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • Access to the latest tools and technology.
  • A chance to be part of exciting projects that push the limits of gaming.

How to Apply:

Please email us your resume, portfolio link, and a brief cover letter explaining why you're the perfect fit for this role.
